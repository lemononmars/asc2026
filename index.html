Welcome to the official website of the 7th Asian Sudoku Championship (ASC).

Info

  • Date: 7-9 February 2026
  • Location: Rembrandt Hotel Bangkok, Bangkok, Thailand (map)
  • Divisions:
    • Individual
      • U8
      • U10
      • U12
      • U15
      • U18
      • Casual
      • Master
    • Team
      • Casual
      • Master
    • Restriction
    • U8: Under 8 years old (born on or after 1 January 2018)
    • U10: Under 10 years old (born on or after 1 January 2016)
    • U12: Under 12 years old (born on or after 1 January 2014)
    • U15: Under 15 years old (born on or after 1 January 2011)
    • U18: Under 18 years old (born on or after 1 January 2008)
    • Open: No age restriction
    • Participants ranked 1-100 in the WPF Sudoku Grand Prix 2024 or 2025, must compete in the open division, regardless of their age.
    • Participants may compete in any higher division (e.g. a 14-year-old participant may choose the U18 division.)
